The Sundowner Sams is a chapter of the National Good Sam Club, located in Richmond, Va., with members in the surrounding area. Our members are working, as well as retired, couples and singles. We welcome any members who enjoy camping, traveling, good fun and fellowship!

Our chapter cuts out coupons for our military families abroad. At Christmas time, we collect food for the food bank. There may be other projects as we see the need.

Our chapter was starte in 1978, one of the first chapters in the state of Virginia. We welcome new members to continue our best traditions and create new ones while enjoying the fellowship of club camping.
